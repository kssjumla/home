About

Karnali Secondary(Anamnagar) School is a multicultural community in the foothills of the Himalayas that inspires in each student a passion for learning, the confidence and competence to pursue their dreams, and the commitment to serve as a compassionate global citizen and leader, who is a steward of the environment.

Karnali Secondary School(KSS) Anamnagar School Jumla

Karnali Secondary School(KSS) is a community-owned educational institution located in Chandannath Municipality, Karnali, Karnali Province of Nepal. This school is affiliated to the National Examinations Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education, Nepal. It offers Ten plus Two (10+2) programs under Management and Education Streams and also offers Diploma in Geomatics Engineering programs under the Management of the CTEVT.

Karnali Secondary School, provides a Primary level to 10+2 (Secondary level) and CTEVT programs with minimal fee structures. It also offers scholarships scheme for brilliant, deserving and the economically backward students of the society. Karnali Secondary School (KSS) provides Counseling, Sports, Library, Multimedia, Educational tours, Labs, Conference, and scholarship facilities.
